Put your review screenshots in this folder.

Naming convention per service (see js/data.js for the full list):
  prepaid-ads-1.jpg, prepaid-ads-2.jpg, ...
  fix-ads-1.jpg, fix-ads-2.jpg, ...
  audience-targeting-1.jpg, ...
  tiktok-setup-1.jpg, ...
  ads-management-1.jpg, ...
  virtual-sessions-1.jpg, ...
  ig-recovery-1.jpg, ...
  cac-registration-1.jpg, ...

After saving an image here, open js/data.js, find that service,
and add the filename to its "reviews" array. That's it.
